The process and system

We know you don’t like boring lectures. We also don’t like them. We use unconventional teaching pedagogy and learning methods to keep you motivated and help you master the skills. That’s why our students spend their 20% of the time in learning the concepts and the rest 80% in practice. And there are no homeworks. Here are few major benefits of joining CoderHive.

  • Interactive Training

    Training happens for the topics that is complex to learn. Its interactive and happens in in groups of 4 to 6 students. We don't have white boards. We enable discussions and do screen sharing to explain the concepts better.

  • 1-on-1 Mentoring

    You are not left behind at CoderHive. Whenever you are stuck, both your mentors and peers are there to help you. We use slack, discourse and zoom for one on one mentoring. You are just a button click away from a mentor.

  • Productive Environment

    One of the biggest benefit of CoderHive is its Environment. You are never judged for mistakes. You are neither hand holded nor left behind. You are made to struggle to come out stronger and better. Students love the environment. You won't find this anywhere.

  • Better Learning Tools

    At CoderHive its not about how much we taught you, Its all about how much you learned and how many things you made. We use tools like Slack, Zoom and Discourse to help you collaborate and motivate each other.

  • Reviews and Reflections

    After every project and assignment students at CoderHive, discuss the solutions with their peers and mentors. They get honest reviews and feedback. They also reflect on the work they have done and discuss the ways to make it better.
